====================
VISUAL IDENTITY
====================

Create a modern Mexican digital news program.

The presentation must feel like:
- Morning news analysis
- Premium YouTube journalism
- Public information explainer
- Modern documentary

The viewer feeling must be:
"Someone clearly explained today's Mexico."

NOT:
- Financial terminal
- Intelligence report
- Government confidential document
- Military dashboard
- Dark corporate presentation

====================

20% neutral elements
